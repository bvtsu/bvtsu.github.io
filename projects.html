Short Linear Motif Evolution pipeline (SLiME)

2018-present

Shell, Python, WDL-based workflow to find short linear motifs (SLiMs) within eukaryotic genomes and map them to orthogonal evolutionary and protein expression datasets.

Led to the novel characterization of three innate immune factors:

  • Tsu BV, Beierschmitt CM, Ryan AP, Agarwal R, Mitchell PS, Daugherty MD. Diverse viral proteases activate the NLRP1 inflammasome. eLife (2021). doi: 10.7554/eLife.60609.
  • Tsu BV, Agarwal R, Gokhale NS, Ryan AP, Castro LK, Beierschmitt CM, Fay EJ, Kulsuptrakul J, Turcotte E, Vance RE, Hyde JL, Savan R, Mitchell PS, Daugherty MD. In preparation.
  • Beierschmitt CM, et al. In preparation.
