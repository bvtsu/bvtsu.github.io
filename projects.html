Short Linear Motif Evolution pipeline (SLiME)

2017-present (Active)

Public Access: Google Colaboratory
Shell, Python-based workflow within Google Colaboratory to find short linear motifs (SLiMs) within eukaryotic genomes and map them to orthogonal evolutionary and protein expression datasets.
Post-Doc PhD student

Led to the novel characterization of three innate immune factors:
