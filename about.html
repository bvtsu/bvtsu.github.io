About Me

Ambitious hybrid computational and bench molecular biologist with over 6 years of academic experience in pairing novel and pre-built computational pipelines and molecular biology techniques to predict and experimentally validate host-pathogen-driven evolutionary innovations within large eukaryotic genomic datasets.
